Welcome to K&W Cafe! We are a local breakfast shop serving the best coffee on the block. Our hours are very irregular, so please check the "Hours" page for the next day we are open. No reservations required, but please give Willa or Kaley a heads if you will be stopping by.

Menu

  • Tea -- a variety of prepackaged and looseleaf flavors

  • Pastries -- cookies and scones
